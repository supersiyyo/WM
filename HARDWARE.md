# Project Hardware

Below is the list of hardware components used in this streaming project.

## 1. Camera
**ANNKE 1080p Security Camera 4-in-1 CCTV Bullet Wired Cam**
- **Type**: AHD/TVI/CVI/CVBS, Analog Surveillance Video Add-On Black Camera for Outdoor Use
- **Features**: 100ft Clear Night Vision
- **Model**: E200
- *(Note: Power Adapter Not Included)*

## 2. Video Converter
**Groza TVI/CVI/AHD to HDMI Converter**
- **Output**: Full HD 4K 720P/ 1080P/ 3MP/ 4MP/ 5MP/ 8MP BNC to HDMI Video Adapter
- **Description**: 
  - Allows you to seamlessly convert an TVI, CVI, AHD, CVBS signal to 720/1080P HDMI signal.
  - The HDMI Video output is 1080p/720P@60Hz resolution (selectable by switch).
  - Includes a BNC Video Loop Out port to connect multiple monitors. 
  - Restores non-loss HD images, maintaining brightness, contrast, and color.
  - Supports long transmission distance (500-700 meters over coax cable).
  - Widely used for home monitoring, shopping malls, traffic monitoring, etc.
- **Includes**: 1x Converter, 1x 5V/1A Power Adapter and Cable, 1x User Manual.

## 3. Video Capture Card
**Guermok Video Capture Card**
- **Type**: 4K USB 3.0 HDMI to USB-C Capture Card for Streaming
- **Performance**: Streams at 1080P 60FPS
- **Compatibility**: iPad, Mac OS, Windows, Quest 3, OBS, PS5/4, Switch, Xbox, Cameras
- **Color**: Silver

## 4. Processing Unit
**Raspberry Pi 5 (CanaKit Edition)**
- Handles the FFmpeg encoding, Nginx RTMP server routing, and network streaming processes.
